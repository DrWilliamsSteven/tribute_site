Examples of QVs code

Historians have discovered many of the Queen's notebooks where she wrote code. Many of them can be found now in the Royal Archives. Some of the most famous code is in the language of A#, an early precursor to C#, and in M, an early precursor to R. Some notable examples are below.

  • QV is credited with inventing the UNIX shortcut: type mkdir followed by the name of the directory.
  • Further, the concept of a CAPS LOCK key is found in her early diaries.
